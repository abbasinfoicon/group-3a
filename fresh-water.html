Fresh Water and Supplies Delivery

We can deliver to ships at any locations in Thailand

Our P&I covered barge can deliver fresh water starting with minimum of 100 MT and up to 200 MT per trip.

It can also deliver various supplies with very limited restriction of weight. Spare parts, cables, chains, etc … can be easily and safely transported.

The barge has a deck space of almost 150 square meters (8 x 18 meters) and is having 8 tanks for fresh water transport.
