Commercial Divers

Our diving branch can attend ships at any locations in Thailand

We offer services of Commercial Divers in Thailand. Our qualified Commercial Divers, accredited by major Classification Societies, can perform all inspection tasks such as (but not limited to ) :

  • Underwater still digital picture and video inspections
  • Hull anti fouling assessment
  • Hull damage assessing and survey, estimation of extent and costs of repairs

Our Commercial Divers in Thailand can also perform underwater hull cleaning and propeller polishing while ship is still working (either loading or discharging) thus avoiding important loss of time and dramatic gain on ship’s performance and consumption. Most of ships calling in Thailand ports for a long period (usually around 30 days for any ship loading cargoes such as rice) will probably call at Singapore to have an underwater hull cleaning and propeller polishing performed before starting the one month long voyage that will take the ship to destination. A dirty hull will create up to 30% over consumption of bunkers and a loss of around 30% speed. It can also, by trying to keep up to the indicated speed on the Charter parties, cause the engine to overheat and lead to serious mechanical damages. With our services the ship can have an underwater hull cleaning and propeller polishing while under commercial operations in Thailand and, thus, not lose any day for this.

Our rates are very competitive compared to neighboring countries and the level of service is at the maximum possible level.

Our Commercial Divers in Thailand are equipped with communication systems (Divers to divers, Divers to surface) as well as latest CCTV equipment so that one supervisor can follow the whole operation from the deck and any ship’s officer can witness as well.

The underwater hull cleaning and propeller polishing is done by way of the latest hydraulic brush karts and hand held tools for polishing propellers.

Additionally, our Commercial Divers in Thailand can perform all other tasks that may be required by a ship such as but not limited to :

  • Ropes removals
  • Chests cleaning
  • Rudder repairs
  • Propellers repairs or replacement
  • Anchors repairs or replacement
  • Anchor chains repairs
  • Anodes changing
  • Underwater welding
  • And more…

Please do not hesitate to contact us as we shall certainly have the Commercial Diver in Thailand that you need to do the job you require.
